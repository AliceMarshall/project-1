1. Ships can only be placed horizontally or vertically across grid spaces, and can't hang over the outer grid boundary. It will return 'Try Again'
  2. Ships are not allowed to share a space with another ship.
  3. The left board is your board to place ships into.
  4. When you have placed all ships, you will be directed to 'Start Game !'
  5. When you 'miss' or 'hit' a ship, the square will appear white or red respectively, and alert you below.
  6. The winner is the first player to sink their opponent's ships before all of their own are sunk.
